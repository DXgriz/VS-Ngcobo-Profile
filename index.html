Profile Background

Who am I?

Hi!
This is Vuyani Ngcobo, a TUT Computer Science graduate - 2024.
I am thrilled to have this opportunity to share a bit about myself with you.

I am just a boy from Empangeni, deep rural KZN trying to make a contribution to the evolution of technology and simultaneously change peoples lives for better, solving their daily problems using the latter.

My Values

In life, I find excitement in exploring new possibilities and pushing boundaries. Whether its delving into innovative projects or embracing unexpected challenges, I believe in the power of continuous growth, and I believe in myself and my capabilities.

I am a technology enthusiast. I am curious and ambitious. Its a side of me that adds a dash of creativity and uniqueness to my perspective. I have been exposed to various environments in the field of ICT, and i have been lucky to be involved with different groups of people whom i have learnt a lot from tackling day to day problems. I know my career is just starting now, and i am eager to learn and share my knowledge and skills and contribute to solving problems in the world through ICT.

Why ICT or Technology

So, why Technology?
Well, its not just about a career opportunity; Its about joining a community that resonates with my values. Technology caught my eye not only for its impressive track record and reviews from the community of alumni. But also for its dynamic approach to focus on scarce skills development which i believe is the the breakthrough the youth of SA needs in this job competitive economy. I am eager to immense myself in this environment and learn from the wealth of experiences that technology offers.

Future Aspirations and Career

As i look towards the future, i am excited about being the best data engineer. well, its not just about the job. Data engineering is the backbone of data-driven decision-making, transforming raw information into valuable insights that drive progress and innovation, so its about contributing meaningfully, making positive impact and continuously evolving in the ever changing landscape.

Educational Background

I started attending school in 2003, at UFasimba Primary School, which forms the base which is my elementary school education, up until I graduated in 2010 (Grade 7). During that stage I had been actively engaging and excelling in most academic activities, and extra-mural activities. I have always been a hard worker. I then started my high school education at Nomyaca High School in 2011 up until 2015 that was my matriculation year. in 2016 I enrolled at University of Zululand for a BSC in Applied Mathematics and Mathematics, where despite all the efforts I put in my academics, I unfortunately dropped out. That was not the end of my journey, through all the difficulties I managed to enroll at Tshwane University of Technology (2021) in pursuit of my Diploma in Computer Science, which I was able to conquer in record time (2023), with very strong academic record. This is NOT the end of my journey, as I am planning to further my studies beyond that.
